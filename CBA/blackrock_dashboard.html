05 · Risk Matrix

Risk Assessment & Mitigation

Identified risk factors with probability/impact assessment and pre-defined mitigation strategies. CBA's hybrid model (tourism + industrial workshop + digital platform) provides natural revenue diversification.

HIGH · Probability: Medium

Political & Regulatory Risk

Venezuela's political landscape may affect operations, permits, and foreign currency access.

Mitigation: Structure investment through international holding. Revenue denominated in USD. Legal framework reviewed by local counsel.
HIGH · Probability: Low

Infrastructure & Logistics

Intermittent utilities, road conditions, and supply chain limitations in coastal Venezuela.

Mitigation: On-site generator and water storage. Solar panel roadmap. Local supplier network pre-established.
MEDIUM · Probability: Medium

Occupancy Below Projection

Tourism demand may fall below 60% occupancy assumption in early years.

Mitigation: Revenue diversification: workshop income + Renace digital platform + fishing/cultural tours. Breakeven at only 40% occupancy.
MEDIUM · Probability: Low

Currency & FX Exposure

Venezuelan bolivar volatility affecting local operating costs.

Mitigation: All prices in USD. International payment rails via PayPal/crypto. Local costs represent only 45% of revenue.
LOW · Probability: Low

Natural Events

Hurricane season or coastal weather events affecting operations.

Mitigation: Container structure is hurricane-resistant by design. Insurance coverage included in operational budget. Seasonal pricing model.
LOW · Probability: Low

Partnership Dissolution

Disagreement among 4 founding partners affecting operations.

Mitigation: Formal shareholders agreement in place. Defined exit and buyout clauses. Investor board seat as governance oversight.
